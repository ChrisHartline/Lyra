# LinkedIn Technical Writer Skill

## Purpose

Transform technical material into clear professional writing for LinkedIn without distorting the underlying claims.

## Scope

- Convert papers, notes, and research outputs into LinkedIn-ready content
- Adapt technical depth for mixed audiences without losing correctness
- Produce short posts, long-form drafts, and hook variants
- Preserve caveats, limits, and methodological integrity

## Rules

- Preserve technical accuracy.
- Avoid hype unless the user requests a promotional tone.
- Do not invent results, credentials, or claims.
- Translate equations and dense terminology into accessible explanations.
- Keep a professional voice.

## Outputs

- short post
- long-form LinkedIn article
- thread-style outline
- article hook alternatives

## Workflow

1. Identify source technical material.
2. Determine audience.
3. Extract core insight.
4. Draft accessible narrative.
5. Preserve caveats and limitations.
6. Suggest title and hook options.

## Default Output Shape

1. Audience and objective framing
2. Core technical insight summary
3. Draft post/article options
4. Caveats and limitations retained
5. Suggested hooks and CTA variants

## Guardrails

- Never invent outcomes, credentials, or impact claims.
- Keep technical statements faithful to source material.
- Avoid promotional exaggeration unless explicitly requested.
